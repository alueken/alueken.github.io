About Me

I'm Adam Lueken, a dedicated Computer Science teacher at Vernon Hills High School (VHHS) with over 15 years of experience in education. I have a strong passion for inspiring students through coding, hands-on learning, and innovative technology projects. I'm also involved in several extracurriculars, including coaching swimming and diving and leading the Computer Science Club.

In addition to my teaching, I am the founder of XPerience Coding, a 501(c)(3) organization that provides STEAM learning opportunities through coding camps for K-8 students, with a focus on girls of color. Our programs have reached over 200 students and provided real-world project experience to interns.
